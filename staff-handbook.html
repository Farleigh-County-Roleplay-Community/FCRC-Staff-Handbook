Policies & Code of Conduct

All staff must adhere to ER:LC roleplay rules, maintain professionalism, and respect other members.

  • No FRP, GTA Driving, or offensive behavior.
  • Follow chain of command at all times.
  • Maintain confidentiality of internal information.

Instructor: Explain policies clearly and give examples of violations and consequences.

Ranks & Promotions

Staff ranks from lowest to highest: Recruit, Officer, Sergeant, Lieutenant, Captain, Director.

RankResponsibilities
RecruitObserve training and follow instructions
OfficerPatrols, minor admin tasks
SergeantSupervise junior staff
LieutenantLead operations, assign tasks
CaptainOversee departments
DirectorManage entire server operations

Instructor: Explain promotion criteria and responsibilities for each rank.

Communication Rules

  • Use clear, professional language.
  • Always announce priorities and updates.
  • Private messages must remain confidential.
  • Report issues through proper channels.

Instructor: Demonstrate correct use of communication commands.

Emergency Procedures

  • Medical emergencies: call EMS and notify supervisors immediately.
  • Server outages: follow IT escalation procedures.
  • Conflict resolution: separate involved parties, report to Director.

Instructor: Review each procedure and perform short drills if needed.

Training & Guidelines

Training includes theoretical knowledge and practical exercises. Guidelines include:

  • Patrol routes and responsibilities
  • Driving tests
  • Command practice
  • Scenario handling

Instructor: Follow step-by-step training procedures and record results.

FAQ

  • Q: What if a trainee fails a test? A: They must redo after review.
  • Q: How to report misconduct? A: Use internal reporting forms.
  • Q: Maximum trainees per session? A: 10 trainees.
